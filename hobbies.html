Fashion and Thrifting

I love experimenting with fashion and thrifting my favorite pieces. I've been thrifting since middle school, and it's my favorite way to find unique clothes. Before I decided on my current major, I debated going into fashion design. While I don't think that major/career path is for me, fashion will always be something I experiment with in my free time!
